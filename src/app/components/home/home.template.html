Our Mission

Featured Projects

WNTR
Water Network Tool for Resilience
developed by the Environmental Protection Agency

A python package designed to simulate and analyze resilience of water distribution networks.

 
ITS ODE
Intelligent Transportation Systems Operational Data Environment
developed by the Department of Transportation

A real-time virtual data router that ingests and processes operational data from various connected devices including vehicles, infrastructure, and traffic management centers and distributes it to other devices and subscribing transportation management applications.

 
WALKOFF
developed by the National Security Agency

Enables security teams to integrate apps, workflows, and analytics tools that would otherwise need to be created from scratch.
